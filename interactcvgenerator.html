Your CV is Ready!

Follow these steps to host your new interactive CV online for free using GitHub Pages.

1 Create a GitHub Account & Repository

If you don't have a GitHub account, sign up for free. Then, create a new repository by clicking the "+" icon in the top-right and selecting "New repository". Name it something like `my-interactive-cv`.

2 Upload Your CV File

In your new repository, click the "Add file" button and choose "Upload files". Drag and drop the `interactcv.html` file you just downloaded into the designated area and click "Commit changes".

3 Enable GitHub Pages

Go to your repository's "Settings" tab. In the left sidebar, click on "Pages". Under "Branch", select `main` from the dropdown menu and click "Save".

4 Share Your Live CV!

GitHub will provide you with a public URL (like `https://your-username.github.io/my-interactive-cv/`). It might take a minute or two to go live. Once it's ready, you can share this link with anyone!
